# Portfolio V2.3 — Owner Review Checklist

Use this checklist after Codex returns the live V2.3 staging URL.

Do not authorize production based only on a green QA report.

---

# 1. Review environment

Check:

- desktop;
- laptop;
- phone;
- at least one touch device;
- Chrome;
- Safari/WebKit when available;
- reduced motion;
- standard/static mode.

Review the live staging site.

Do not rely only on screenshots.

---

# 2. Five-second test

Open the homepage in a new private window.

After five seconds, answer:

- What is Shailesh’s role?
- What industries?
- Does the site show predictive ML through agentic AI?
- Is the current production focus clear?
- Is the main action obvious?
- Is there a memorable visual?

Fail the direction when the answer requires scrolling or interpretation.

---

# 3. Visual identity

Ask:

- Does this feel bespoke?
- Does it feel modern and elegant?
- Does the sci-fi influence feel professional?
- Does glass create depth and hierarchy?
- Is the site visually different from V2.2?
- Is the visual identity consistent without making every page identical?
- Are advanced effects meaningful?
- Does any part look like a purchasable template?
- Does any part look like generic AI wallpaper?
- Is neon/lime overused?
- Is typography expressive and readable?

---

# 4. Animation and interaction

Verify:

- hero interaction;
- career/breadth interaction;
- current claims-agent interaction;
- Projects Lab interaction;
- Research interaction;
- card-to-detail transitions;
- page transitions;
- hover/focus;
- mobile controls.

Ask:

- Did the interaction teach me something?
- Did I understand cause and effect?
- Did it remain responsive?
- Could I pause or avoid motion?
- Did motion become repetitive?
- Did animation delay the content?

At least three interactions should remain memorable after closing the tab.

---

# 5. Professional hierarchy

Confirm the site prioritizes:

1. Current production agentic AI
2. Predictive ML
3. Healthcare platform/product leadership
4. Document intelligence/RAG
5. Earlier scientific foundations
6. Open source
7. Research and recognition

Open source must not overshadow professional work.

Context and harness engineering must not become the identity.

---

- biomedical research;
- sensor/time-series work;

- OCR/RAG;
- agentic AI;
- open source.

- supporting copy;

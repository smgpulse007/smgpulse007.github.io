## Progressive-enhancement contract

- identity, headings, claims, qualifications, links, project labels, and research sources render in HTML;
- SVG surfaces include textual titles/descriptions or adjacent text;
- JavaScript adds playback, selection, filtering, and tab behavior;
- reduced motion removes continuous animation and smooth scrolling;
- reduced transparency replaces glass instruments with solid surfaces;
- forced colors removes material assumptions;
- a failure of JavaScript, WebGL, or animation must not remove the professional narrative.

## Editorial guardrails

- never use `7K documents/day`;
- use `7K-case backlog cleared`;
- use `20% automated measure-closure improvement`;
- keep current claims-effort and earlier payer review-time outcomes distinct;
- attach approximate and program-level qualifications;
- do not imply private source code, prompts, screenshots, policies, or production data are public;
- do not claim a public reference implementation is the private employer system;
- preserve team attribution for recognition;
- distinguish external research from authored publications;
- distinguish portfolio tier from repository health and GitHub archive state.
